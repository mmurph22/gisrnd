Lab 6

Task 1: Get the Embedded map for the Foliage Collector

In this map, we collected foliage areas around my home property, recording the top height in meters, classify by type, and a reflectance value. In some cases, pictures were taken as samples. The map is classified by types (Evergreen, Maple, Landscape, Bushes).

Reflectance, measures how well the foliage line reflects sunlight, and hence gives shade. The higher the score, the more dense the canopy is, and so can reflect light better. (This was scaled from 0 to 1, but could have use a 1 to 10 scale). This reflectance measure can also be used to measure any electromagnetic wave (radio, cell phone, ground sensor, survey total station, etc.) to predict how much interference the canopy will produce.
