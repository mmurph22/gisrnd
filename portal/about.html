My Profile

Software Developer (Contractor) for US Army Command, Control, Communications, Computing, Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (C4ISR) at Aberdeen Proving Ground (APG). I primarily develop software to support R/D and fielding of Electromagnetic Spectrum Analysis and Electronic Warfare. I have a BS in Surveying and Geodetic Science Engineering from The Ohio State University, from way back in the 20th Century (1994). I started as a GPS surveyor, then moved to software developer in 1997. Since then I have developed for military GIS applications, ecommerce, Medical Imaging, sensor/radar C2, Signal Corp C2, Intel Systems Engineering, Spectrum Analysis/Modeling/Simulation (M&S).My interest (beyond C4ISR) is to explore stronger GIS solutions (and developers) where GIS is the primary product, especially in areas of analytics and automation.
